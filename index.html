Flyer2Cal Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026

Flyer2Cal is a macOS app that helps you turn flyer images into Calendar event drafts.

What Flyer2Cal Processes

Flyer2Cal may process:

  • Flyer images you open, drag into the app, or send through the macOS Services menu
  • OCR text extracted from those flyer images
  • Event fields derived from that OCR text, such as title, date, time, location, and notes
  • Local app settings, such as menu bar preferences and behavior toggles
  • StoreKit purchase and restore state for Flyer2Cal Pro

How Flyer2Cal Uses Data

  • Flyer OCR is performed locally on your Mac using Apple frameworks.
  • Event parsing is also performed locally on your Mac.
  • When you choose Add to Calendar, Flyer2Cal creates a local .ics file and opens Apple Calendar with a prefilled draft.
  • The final event is saved only if you choose to save it in Calendar.

What Flyer2Cal Does Not Do

  • Flyer2Cal does not operate its own backend for flyer processing.
  • Flyer2Cal does not upload flyer images or OCR text to any server.
  • Flyer2Cal does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or user-account tracking.

Optional Settings

By default, Flyer2Cal keeps Calendar exports minimal. You can optionally enable:

  • Including extracted OCR text in Calendar notes
  • Attaching the flyer image to the Calendar event
  • Archiving a local copy of the flyer to a folder you select

These options are off by default and only applied when you turn them on.

Local Storage

Flyer2Cal stores some information locally on your Mac, including:

  • App preferences in local macOS app storage
  • Free conversion credit state in local secure storage (Keychain)
  • Temporary .ics export files used to open Calendar
  • Optional archived flyer copies if you enable archiving

If you enable flyer archiving, Flyer2Cal uses the folder you select and stores access using macOS security-scoped bookmarks.

Purchases

Flyer2Cal Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited conversions for the functional lifetime of Flyer2Cal on supported macOS versions. It does not guarantee compatibility with future macOS versions or future major versions of the app.

Purchases and restores are handled through Apple StoreKit and your App Store account. Apple may process data related to those transactions under Apple's own privacy policy.

Data Sharing

Flyer2Cal does not sell your personal data. Flyer2Cal only shares data with Apple services when needed for:

  • StoreKit purchase and restore flows
  • Opening the event draft in Apple Calendar

Security

Flyer2Cal runs in the macOS app sandbox and validates incoming files before processing. The app limits accepted image sizes and dimensions to reduce risk from malformed or unexpectedly large inputs.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in any updated versions and this document.
